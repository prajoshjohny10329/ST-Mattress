Brandz Zone - Leading Supplier of Premium Branded Products in
Kottarakkara

Welcome to Brandz Zone, your premier destination for premium branded products in Mannarkad, Palakkad. Founded in 1995 by Mr. Ganesh, we have established ourselves as one of the largest direct dealers in the region. Our expertise spans a comprehensive range of products and services, including electrical, plumbing, sanitary ware, fancy lights, and much more. As a complete interior design, consulting, and execution firm, our talented designers are dedicated to bringing your vision to life.

We proudly represent over 130 global brands, including Kohler, Grohe, Jaquar, Hettich, Hafele, and Finolex. Our extensive collection is showcased in our showroom, featuring live displays of luxury building materials from top international brands from Germany, Italy, Holland, the US, and France.

Mr. Ganesh

Founder

Our Mission

At Brandz Zone, our mission is to empower spaces with premium products and innovative solutions that meet the diverse needs of our customers. We are committed to delivering unparalleled service, building lasting relationships, and providing a seamless shopping experience.

Our Vision

We envision a world where quality and craftsmanship are accessible to all. Our goal is to continue expanding our product range, enhancing our services, and maintaining our reputation as a trusted partner for both individuals and businesses in the construction and interior design industries.
